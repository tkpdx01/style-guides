## Style Summaries

### neo-brutalist

**Visual Identity:** Hard shadows, bold dark borders, warm creamy backgrounds, rounded typography

**Tech Stack:** Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Radix UI, Nunito + Space Mono fonts

**Key Classes:**
- `shadow-hard` / `shadow-hard-lg` - Solid offset shadows
- `hover-lift` - Interactive lift on hover
- `border-2 border-border` - Bold dark borders
- `rounded-xl` / `rounded-2xl` - Large border radius

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Orange `hsl(24 90% 50%)`
- Background: Cream `hsl(45 40% 96%)`
- Border: Near-black `hsl(30 20% 15%)`
